About

Olivia Rodrigo is a twenty-one year old American singer and actress. Her rise to fame can be attributed to her role in the 2019 Disney+ series High School Musical: The Musical: The Series (Wikipedia contributors, n.d.).

Early Life

Rodrigo was born Olivia Isabel Rodrigo on February 20, 2003 in Murrieta, California, to Chris and Jennifer Rodrigo. She is an only child and of Filipino descent (Wikipedia contributors, n.d.).

Early Career

Rodrigo's acting rebut was in 2015 at the age of twelve. She portrayed the lead role of Grace Thomas in the film An American Girl: Grace Steers Up Success She then went to land a role in the Disney Channel show Bizaardark in 2016 (Wikipedia contributors, n.d.).

Recent Years

As of 2025, Rodrigo's young career has been full of accomplishments. Her music has won her several awards, including the Hollywood Music in Media Award for Best Original Song in a Sci-Fi, Fantasy or Horror Film in 2023. She has gone on two massively successful tours. In June of this year, she is set to headline the 2025 BST Hyde Park Show (Wikipedia contributors, n.d.).
